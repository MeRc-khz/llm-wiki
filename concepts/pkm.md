# Personal Knowledge Management (PKM)

**Personal Knowledge Management (PKM)** is a disciplined process of collecting, organizing, synthesizing, and retrieving knowledge for personal or professional growth.

Traditionally, PKM has been a manual and cognitively demanding task—requiring systems like Zettelkasten or P.A.R.A. and tools like Obsidian. 

With the emergence of LLMs, PKM workflows are shifting towards co-creation, where the human directs the high-level research and structure, and AI agents perform the heavy lifting of indexing, linking, and formatting through patterns like the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] or standard [[rag]] retrieval.
