# Ballademix: The Catalog of an Unknown Rap God

## Overview
"Ballademix: the catalog of a unknown Rap God" is a Solana-based Audio/Video NFT collection and exclusive web portal. The project merges cryptographic puzzle-contests, algorithmic music stem remixing, and collaborative video reconstruction.

## Key Features & Mechanics

### 1. The Solana NFT Collection
- **Supply:** 9 unique Audio/Video NFTs in total.
- **Vocal Foundation:** Based on the catalog of an "unknown Rap God."
- **Visuals (Looping Video Chunks):** Each NFT contains a randomly extracted, silent "chunk" of the *Paperchase* (or *Papachasa*) music video. These chunks loop continuously like a meme, acting as the visual cover art.

### 2. Algorithmic Audio Remixing
- **Stem separation:** The original vocal track stems are isolated.
- **Suno Remix Stems:** Integrates Suno AI remix stems of the vocals.
- **Generative Beat Engine:** A custom beat generator algorithmically constructs a randomized, unique instrumental beat for each individual NFT owner, making every audio track unique to its holder.

### 3. Integrated Cryptographic Contests
- Each of the 9 NFTs has an active contest attached to it.
- **The Wallet Contest:** One specific NFT is paired with a hidden crypto wallet.
- **The Mechanic:** Collectors compete to guess/discover the wallet's passphrase.
- **The Reward:** The successful solver receives **5% of the total revenue generated from the sale** of that specific NFT.

### 4. Exclusive Portal: bzzrrr.link
NFT owners can log in to a gated web portal hosted at `bzzrrr.link` using their Solana wallet signature.
- **remix Collaboration:** Users can discuss, trade, and share their unique tracks and generated remix beats.
- **The Video Wall:** The portal aggregates the video chunks owned by active portal members. Each user's silent *Paperchase* clip is added to a unified "video wall."
- **Democratic Video Unlock:** Once a threshold of unique chunks is reached in the portal, the full, audio-synced *Paperchase* music video is unlocked and made playable for all active NFT holders.
