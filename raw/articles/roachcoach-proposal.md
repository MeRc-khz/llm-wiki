# RoachCoach.com Company Description and Proposal

## Overview
RoachCoach.com is a cutting-edge Progressive Web Application (PWA) company based out of the Third Ward in Houston, TX. It is designed for Foodies and Food Truck operators to track a truck's location and allow for advanced customer engagement.

## Features & Mechanics

### 1. Advanced OSM GPS Positioning
- Full-screen OpenStreetMap (OSM) on the home page.
- Highly performant real-time GPS tracking of food truck locations.
- Route navigation showing the foodie the exact route to selected food trucks once a choice is made.

### 2. The Bio Card & "Tinder-style" Swiping Game
- **Area/Zip Code Search:** Users enter a zip code or circle an area directly on the map.
- **Card Stack Transition:** Circling an area brings up a stack of "Food Truck Bio Cards" (baseball-card style stats: cuisine, number of sales, number of locations, ratings, etc.) for the trucks in that circled area.
- **Swipe Interactions:** Users swipe right for "yes" and left for "no" through the card stack.
- **Route Plotting:** Once the user reaches the end of the stack, the app transitions back to the map and draws optimized travel itineraries/routes to all selected ("yes") trucks.

### 3. Cutting-Edge Creator & Operator Tools
- **Live Streams:** Truck owners can go live directly on their Bio Card (camera inside the truck, kit sold separately by RoachCoach.com).
- **Interactive Audience Polls:** Truck owners can poll their foodie followers in real-time ("Who's coming through?", "What's the best cuisine?", etc.).
- **Comments Section:** Both owners and users can leave comments.
- **Vlog & Blog:** Dedicated spaces for truck owners to wax poetic, post vlog clips, and share updates.

### 5. Interactive UX
- **Intro Sequence:** A styled entrance sequence page showcasing the logo/logo animation with sound.
- **Header Bar:** Slightly translucent with:
  - Hamburger menu on the left.
  - RoachCoach.com logo in the middle.
  - Left-justified horizontal link list: `Blog | Contact | SELL`.
